We aim to acknowledge serious complaints within a reasonable timeframe. Complex issues (for example involving multiple domains or third-party data) may take longer to fully investigate.

What this contact hub cannot provide

There are important limits to what we can help with from ContactUs.bio:

  • We do not provide personalised medical advice or diagnosis.
  • We cannot override decisions made by your doctor or healthcare provider.
  • We cannot give urgent crisis support – please use local emergency services.
  • We do not give individual investment or financial advice.

For medical concerns, always contact your own doctor or local healthcare system first.
